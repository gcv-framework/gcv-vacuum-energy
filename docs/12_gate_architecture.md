## Route summary

The Governance Bridge is not one branch among others. It is the GCV source-assignment translation layer. Starting from the IR GR-exact backbone, where the strictly spacetime-constant vacuum mode is excluded from the local gravitational source and Λ_eff appears as a flux-fixed integration constant, the bridge rewrites the required landing condition in UV-facing language.

A candidate UV-side realization is admissible only if it realizes this source assignment without reintroducing a freely running local vacuum-energy coupling.

The immediate output of G0 is the GR-exact landed branch. The observed-branch candidate is the later object, after downstream gates.

## Claim boundary

This record does not claim:

1. a new CCP proof;
2. a new Governance Bridge proof;
3. a new numerical value-selection scan;
4. a new UV construction;
5. a global measure over all branches;
6. a unique UV completion;
7. a UV theorem forcing Λ > 0;
8. a full nonlinear galaxy-population theorem.

It does claim a narrower organizational result: inside the GCV paper stack, the Governance Bridge is the source-assignment translation layer that defines lawful UV-to-IR crossing into the GR-exact branch. After that crossing, the remaining programme is branch filtration.
